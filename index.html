About

Founded in 2015, nanoBike has become one of the world's leading electric bike producer. In 2018, we aquired Boosted Bike.co and incorperated their designs into ours to create what is now the worlds best built bike tech. With innovations like our nano frequencies, Millimeter Space sensing, built in GPS, nanoBike has won many awards and most recently, was named Best Electric Bike Company of 2019.
